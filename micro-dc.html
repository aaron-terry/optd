Micro-DC

Micro-DC is an all-in-one data centre solution in a compact footprint, ideal for edge computing and remote sites...

The system consists of the Facilities Rack which contains all of the cooling, controls and power management. Additional equipment racks are added to create the size of system required depending on IT density and power load. For higher power systems and where redundancy is required, the Slave Rack provides additional cooling and UPS capabilities.

  • Complete solution – just add servers
  • Scalable – with a low cost per kW
  • Quick to install and commission – minimising disruption to your business
  • Environmentally independent – with no heat transfer to the installation environment
  • Exceptional efficiency – with PUE values as low as 1.21*
  • Futureproof – allowing rack densities to be increased as needs change
  • Secure – with remote monitoring and access control

Standard system available in 36U, 42U, and 48U heights, with a footprint of 600 x 1200 mm per rack .

Full airflow management including blank panels.
